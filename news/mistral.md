# Mistral AI

---
date: 2023-04-28
importance: high
headline: Mistral AI founded in Paris
---

Arthur Mensch, Guillaume Lample, and Timothée Lacroix — former Meta and DeepMind researchers — found Mistral AI. The company raises €105M in seed funding, a European record.

---
date: 2023-09-27
importance: high
headline: Mistral 7B released — best open model at its size
---

Mistral releases Mistral 7B via a torrent link with no announcement, outperforming Llama 2 13B on all benchmarks. The unconventional release and strong performance establish Mistral as a serious open-source competitor.

---
date: 2023-12-11
importance: critical
headline: Mixtral 8x7B — mixture of experts goes open-source
---

Mistral releases Mixtral 8x7B, a sparse mixture-of-experts model that matches GPT-3.5 performance while being open-weight. It demonstrates that MoE architectures can deliver frontier performance efficiently.

---
date: 2024-02-26
importance: high
headline: Mistral Large and Le Chat launched
---

Mistral releases its first commercial-grade model, Mistral Large, alongside Le Chat, a free conversational interface. Mistral Large competes with GPT-4 on multilingual and reasoning tasks.

---
date: 2024-06-11
importance: medium
headline: Mistral raises €600M at $6B valuation
---

Mistral AI closes a massive Series B round, becoming one of Europe's most valuable AI startups. General Catalyst and Lightspeed lead the round.

---
date: 2024-07-24
importance: high
headline: Mistral Large 2 released
---

Mistral releases Mistral Large 2, a 123B parameter model competitive with Llama 3.1 405B and GPT-4o on key benchmarks. It supports 80+ languages and a 128K context window.

---
date: 2024-09-18
importance: medium
headline: Pixtral 12B — Mistral's first multimodal model
---

Mistral launches Pixtral 12B, its first vision-language model, capable of understanding images alongside text. It's released as open-source under Apache 2.0.

---
date: 2025-01-29
importance: high
headline: Mistral Small 3 — efficient on-device model
---

Mistral releases Small 3, a 24B parameter model rivaling much larger models on common benchmarks. It's designed for on-device and edge deployment.
