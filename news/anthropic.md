# Anthropic

---
date: 2021-01-28
importance: critical
headline: Anthropic founded by former OpenAI leaders
---

Dario Amodei, Daniela Amodei, and other former OpenAI researchers found Anthropic with a focus on AI safety. The company pursues "constitutional AI" — training models to be helpful, harmless, and honest.

---
date: 2021-05-28
importance: medium
headline: Anthropic raises $124M Series A
---

Anthropic closes a $124 million Series A round led by Jaan Tallinn and others. The funding establishes the company as a serious AI safety-focused lab.

---
date: 2022-12-15
importance: high
headline: Constitutional AI paper published
---

Anthropic publishes its Constitutional AI research, describing a method to train AI assistants using a set of principles rather than human feedback alone. The approach becomes central to Anthropic's alignment strategy.

---
date: 2023-03-14
importance: high
headline: Claude 1 launched
---

Anthropic releases Claude, its first commercial AI assistant, trained using Constitutional AI principles. It competes directly with ChatGPT and offers a 100K token context window.

---
date: 2023-07-11
importance: high
headline: Claude 2 released with improved capabilities
---

Claude 2 launches with stronger coding, math, and reasoning skills plus a 100K context window. It demonstrates significant improvements over the original Claude.

---
date: 2023-09-25
importance: high
headline: Amazon invests up to $4B in Anthropic
---

Amazon announces an investment of up to $4 billion in Anthropic, the largest venture investment in the company's history. Anthropic selects AWS as its primary cloud provider.

---
date: 2024-03-04
importance: critical
headline: Claude 3 family launched — Opus, Sonnet, Haiku
---

Anthropic releases three Claude 3 models spanning different capability and cost tiers. Claude 3 Opus matches or exceeds GPT-4 on key benchmarks, establishing Anthropic as a frontier lab.

---
date: 2024-06-20
importance: high
headline: Claude 3.5 Sonnet released
---

Claude 3.5 Sonnet launches and outperforms Claude 3 Opus at a fraction of the cost and latency. It becomes one of the most capable and popular AI models available.

---
date: 2024-10-22
importance: high
headline: Computer use capability introduced
---

Anthropic launches Claude's computer use ability in public beta, allowing it to interact with desktop applications by viewing screens and controlling mouse and keyboard.

---
date: 2024-11-25
importance: medium
headline: Amazon increases Anthropic investment to $8B
---

Amazon doubles its total investment in Anthropic to $8 billion, deepening the partnership. The deal reinforces Anthropic's position as a leading AI lab.

---
date: 2025-01-09
importance: medium
headline: Claude launches on iPhone and Android
---

Anthropic releases the Claude mobile app, making its AI assistant accessible on smartphones for the first time. The app supports voice and image inputs.

---
date: 2025-02-14
importance: critical
headline: Claude 3.7 Sonnet with extended thinking
---

Anthropic releases Claude 3.7 Sonnet, a hybrid model that can switch between fast responses and extended step-by-step reasoning. It represents a new approach to combining speed with deep thinking.

---
date: 2025-03-01
importance: medium
headline: Claude gets web search capability
---

Anthropic adds a web search feature to Claude, starting with paying users in the United States. The feature allows Claude to access real-time information from the internet for the first time.

---
date: 2025-03-15
importance: high
headline: Anthropic raises $3.5B at $61.5B valuation
---

Anthropic raises $3.5 billion in a Series E funding round led by Lightspeed Venture Partners, achieving a post-money valuation of $61.5 billion. Google also invests an additional $1 billion separately.

---
date: 2025-05-22
importance: inflection
headline: Claude 4 launched — Opus 4 and Sonnet 4
---

Anthropic releases Claude Opus 4 and Claude Sonnet 4 with major improvements in coding and agentic capabilities. Claude Code exits preview to general availability with VS Code and JetBrains integrations. Opus 4 is classified as a "Level 3" safety risk model.

---
date: 2025-08-05
importance: high
headline: Claude Opus 4.1 released
---

Anthropic releases Opus 4.1 with improved capabilities. A new feature allows Opus 4 and 4.1 to end conversations that remain "persistently harmful or abusive" as a last resort after multiple refusals.

---
date: 2025-08-25
importance: medium
headline: Anthropic revokes OpenAI's Claude Code access
---

Anthropic discovers that OpenAI employees have been using Claude Code and revokes their access, calling it "a direct violation of our terms of service." The incident highlights Claude Code's dominance in AI-assisted development.

---
date: 2025-09-15
importance: critical
headline: Anthropic raises $13B at $183B valuation
---

Anthropic completes a massive Series F funding round, raising $13 billion at a $183 billion post-money valuation. The round is co-led by Iconiq Capital, Fidelity, and Lightspeed, with participation from the Qatar Investment Authority.

---
date: 2025-09-29
importance: high
headline: Claude Sonnet 4.5 released
---

Anthropic releases Claude Sonnet 4.5 with a knowledge cutoff of July 2025, improving on the previous Sonnet model across coding and general tasks.

---
date: 2025-10-15
importance: medium
headline: Claude Haiku 4.5 for smaller deployments
---

Anthropic releases Claude Haiku 4.5, a faster and cheaper model targeting smaller companies and rapid-response use cases. It's available on the Claude website and mobile app.

---
date: 2025-11-24
importance: high
headline: Claude Opus 4.5 with Infinite Chats
---

Anthropic releases Opus 4.5 with major improvements in coding and spreadsheet tasks. A new "Infinite Chats" feature eliminates context window limit errors, removing a long-standing limitation of AI assistants.

---
date: 2025-11-25
importance: critical
headline: Nvidia-Microsoft invest $15B in Anthropic
---

Nvidia, Microsoft, and Anthropic announce a partnership deal. Nvidia and Microsoft invest up to $15 billion in Anthropic, while Anthropic commits to buying $30 billion of compute capacity from Microsoft Azure running on Nvidia systems.

---
date: 2025-12-31
importance: high
headline: Anthropic signs $10B round at $350B valuation
---

Anthropic confirms a term sheet for a $10 billion funding round led by Coatue and GIC at a $350 billion valuation, capping an extraordinary year of fundraising.

---
date: 2026-02-05
importance: high
headline: Claude Opus 4.6 released
---

Anthropic releases Opus 4.6 with agent team capabilities and Claude in PowerPoint integration. It becomes the latest stable release in the rapidly evolving Claude model family.

---
date: 2026-02-12
importance: critical
headline: Anthropic raises $30B at $380B valuation
---

Anthropic announces a $30 billion Series G funding round, bringing its post-money valuation to $380 billion. The company also airs Super Bowl LX commercials as part of its "A Time and a Place" marketing campaign.
